Tired of boring forms? Enhance your Contact Form 7 with interactive origami animations, making form-filling fun and engaging!

== Description ==

**Origami For Contact Form 7** transforms standard forms into interactive, visually engaging experiences. Each form field reveals a step of an origami animation—birds, boats, trees, and more—making your forms unique and enjoyable.

**Key Features**:

- **Interactive Origami Animations** – Animated origami figures that respond to form fields being filled out.
- **Compatible with Contact Form 7** – Works seamlessly with all your Contact Form 7 forms. Just add a shortcode, and you're ready to go.
- **Customizable Origami Styles** – Choose from several themed animations (birds, boats, camels, trees), or upload your own custom origami images for a personal touch.
- **Increased User Engagement** – Fun, interactive form animations that make filling out forms enjoyable for users.
- **Custom Settings** – Adjust origami captions, images, and themes directly from the plugin settings page.

**Perfect for any website that uses Contact Form 7!** Whether you run a business, blog, or eCommerce site, you can easily integrate these animations to make form submissions more appealing and engaging for users.

== Benefits of Using Origami for Contact Form 7 ==

1. **Increased Form Completions** – Fun visuals keep users engaged, improving form completion rates.
2. **Easy Customization** – Customize the origami steps and animation types to match your brand or website theme.
3. **Enhanced User Experience** – Create memorable user interactions and avoid boring form designs.

Don't let your forms be dull—give them life with **Origami For Contact Form 7**!

== How It Works ==

1. **Install the Plugin** – Upload the plugin to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or install it directly from the WordPress plugins screen.
2. **Activate the Plugin** – Activate it via the WordPress 'Plugins' screen.
3. **Customize Your Form Animations** – Choose your preferred origami theme and upload custom images from the plugin settings.
4. **Use the Shortcode** – Add the shortcode `[origami_cf7 id="YOUR_FORM_ID"]` to display the origami animation alongside any Contact Form 7 form (replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` with your actual Contact Form 7 form ID).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/`, or install via the WordPress plugin screen.
2. Activate through the 'Plugins' screen.
3. Go to the settings page, select or upload origami images.
4. Use `[origami_cf7 id="YOUR_FORM_ID"]` shortcode in your pages or posts.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I change the animations? =

Visit the plugin settings page to select or upload your own origami images.

= Can I use this with any Contact Form 7 form? =

Yes! Just use the shortcode `[origami_cf7 id="YOUR_FORM_ID"]` with any Contact Form 7 form.

= Can I upload custom images? =

Absolutely! You can upload custom images for each origami step directly from the settings page.
